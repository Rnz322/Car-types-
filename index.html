The Different Car Types & Their Benefits

Hatchback

One of the most popular types of car in the UK is the hatchback... Awesome for light use or city life, and they’re cheaper too.

Saloon

Saloons are almost always 5 doors... They’re all fast, reliable and pretty comfortable to drive.

Estate

They’re extended so they have van-like practicality while still having some of the comfort and performance of smaller vehicles.

SUV

SUV stands for Sports Utility Vehicle... Fast and practical, but not always convenient or affordable.

4×4

4x4s are usually bigger, more off-road built cars with sheer practicality in mind... There’s a big difference.

People Carrier

Built to carry more people than normal without compromising safety... but they definitely do the job.
