The Different Car Types & Their Benefits

When it comes to cars, there are a lot out there. Let’s take a look at a few popular types:

Hatchback

Hatchbacks are popular for their practicality and efficiency. Great for city driving!

Saloon

Saloon cars are spacious, fast, and reliable for long-distance driving.

Estate

Estate cars offer the best of both worlds—extra space and comfort.

SUV

SUVs are versatile and great for families or off-road adventures.

4×4

4x4 vehicles are built for tougher terrains, offering unmatched off-road capabilities.

People Carrier

These cars are ideal for large families or groups, offering space and safety.

Sports

Sports cars are all about speed and performance, perfect for thrill-seekers!
